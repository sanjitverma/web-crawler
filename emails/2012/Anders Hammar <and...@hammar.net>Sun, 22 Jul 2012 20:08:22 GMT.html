No, it's not possible to manage the version of reporting plugins in
the reporting section through pluginManagement.

However, if you're using Maven 3 and maven-site-plugin 3.x, you can
take advantage of the new version resolution:
http://maven.apache.org/plugins/maven-site-plugin/maven-3.html#Version_Resolution
The text doesn't say, but this *might* require that you use the new
configuration format:
http://maven.apache.org/plugins/maven-site-plugin/maven-3.html#New_Configuration_Maven_3_only_no_reports_configuration_inheritance

/Anders
