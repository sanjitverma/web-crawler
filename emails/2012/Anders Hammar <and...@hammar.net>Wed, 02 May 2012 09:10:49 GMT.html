There is no simple conversion. I strongly suggest you first of all
read one of the free Maven books, or similar docs, to get a grip on
what Maven is. Then you need to forget how Ant did things and
re-implement your build with Maven. Maven is different from Ant.
Trying to convert one-to-one from Ant to Maven is the first step to
failure.

And also, please search this list's archive or Internet, there have
been several similar questions before with answers.

/Anders
