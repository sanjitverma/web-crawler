Why would you want to be able swap this value?
settings.xml is a personal settings file.
Either your proxy is on, or it isn't.

I'm stuck behind an NTLM proxy, and whenever I have needed to reach
around our Nexus instance, I just use ntlmaps running locally, edit
the file and keep going.
Just re-edit the file later to turn it off when you need it.

A command line switch isn't going to offer you that much.
